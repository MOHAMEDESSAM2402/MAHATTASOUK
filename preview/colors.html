Colors

The palette is built around a single gradient. Purple calms, pink lights up. Neutrals do nearly all the work; gradient and pure purple carry meaning where they appear.

The Mahatta gradient

Reserved for: CTAs, active tabs, brand marks, price-discount badges, logo container. Do not stretch it behind wide surfaces — it cheapens. If a surface needs the gradient, use --m-grad-soft.

Usage

Gradient (use sparingly)

  • Primary CTAs: "Buy now", "Checkout", "Follow"
  • Logo container, active nav indicator
  • Discount badges (-25%)
  • Wallet balance panel, cart badge
  • NEVER: long headers, full page backgrounds, body text

Solid purple (the workhorse)

  • Active tabs, selected states, focus rings
  • Hyperlinks and "by <influencer>" chips
  • Counts, stats, key numbers on dashboards
  • Progress bars, slider tracks (accent-color)
