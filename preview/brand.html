Voice & tone

Do

  • Arabic first — العربية أولاً. English is a fallback, not a peer.
  • Warm and direct. Speak like a trusted friend recommending something.
  • Use real names of influencers, sellers, cities. Never generic.
  • Numbers in Arabic numerals (٠١٢٣...) when addressing Saudi customers.
  • Prices as "١٢٠ ر.س" never "SAR 120" in Arabic UI.

Don't

  • Stiff corporate phrasing. No "dear valued customer".
  • Crypto/NFT energy. No rocket emoji. No "disrupt".
  • Mixed-language run-on sentences. Pick one, localize the rest.
  • Generic stock phrases like "your one-stop shop for everything".
  • Over-promising logistics. Under-promise, over-deliver.
